## FULL DESCRIPTION (App Store / Google Play)

```
Walsikopi Ordering is the official ordering app for Walsikopi. Browse our full menu, customize your order, and place it — all from your phone. Every order is sent directly to our Facebook Messenger page, where our team personally confirms and processes your order in real time.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Choose Your Order Type
Select how you'd like to receive your order — whether it's Pickup, Delivery, or Dine In. The available options are set up specifically for Walsikopi's ordering process.

2. Browse the Menu
Explore our complete menu organized by categories. Search for your favorite items, view photos, descriptions, and prices. Select variations (like size or flavor) and add-ons to customize your order exactly the way you want it.

3. Add to Cart & Review
Add items to your cart, adjust quantities, add special instructions, and review your order before checking out.

4. Fill in Your Details
Provide the required customer information based on your selected order type (name, phone number, address, etc.). This information is included in your order message so we can process it properly.

5. Select Payment Method
Choose from the available payment methods configured for Walsikopi. Payment details and instructions are shown at checkout.

6. Place Your Order via Messenger
When you tap "Place Order," your complete order is automatically formatted and sent to Walsikopi's Facebook Messenger page. The app opens Messenger for you so you can confirm and communicate directly with our team. This is where your order is officially received and confirmed.

KEY FEATURES

- Full menu browsing with categories, search, photos, and descriptions
- Item customization with variations (sizes, flavors) and add-ons
- Special instructions for individual items
- Cart management with quantity controls
- Multiple order types (Pickup, Delivery, Dine In)
- Multiple payment method options
- Order confirmation sent directly through Facebook Messenger
- Real-time order communication with the Walsikopi team via Messenger
- Order history tracking on your device
- Promotion banners and announcements
- Clean, easy-to-use interface designed for fast ordering

WHY MESSENGER?

At Walsikopi, we believe in personal service. Every order goes through our Facebook Messenger page so that our team can personally confirm your order, answer any questions, and keep you updated on preparation and delivery. This direct communication ensures accuracy and a better experience for every customer.

REQUIREMENTS

- Facebook Messenger app installed on your device (for order confirmation)
- Internet connection

Download now and start ordering from Walsikopi!
```

## APP REVIEW NOTES (For Apple Review Team)

```
This app is the official ordering app for Walsikopi, a food and coffee business.

How the app works:
1. Users browse the Walsikopi menu and add items to their cart
2. Users fill in customer details (name, phone, etc.) at checkout
3. Users select a payment method
4. When the user taps "Place Order," the app generates a formatted order message and opens Facebook Messenger to send it to the Walsikopi business page
5. The Walsikopi team confirms and processes the order through the Messenger conversation

The Facebook Messenger integration is the core order confirmation mechanism for this business. All orders are manually confirmed by the business through Messenger, which is how Walsikopi operates its ordering process.

Facebook Messenger must be installed on the device for order submission to work. If Messenger is not installed, the user is prompted to copy the order message manually.

No demo account is needed — the app is fully functional without login. Simply:
1. Select any order type on the home screen
2. Browse the menu and add items to cart
3. Proceed to checkout
4. The app will attempt to open Messenger with the order details
```
